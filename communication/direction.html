🗣️ Give and follow directions using prepositions and direction verbs

Pattern 1 · in the north/south/east/west of

Pattern 2 · on the north/south/east/west side of
The park is on the north side of the city.
The hospital is on the east side of the street.
Pattern 3 · in the northern/southern/eastern/western part of
The park is in the northern part of the city.
The lake is in the western part of the park.
Pattern 4 · to the north/south/east/west of
The park is to the north of the city.
The café is to the south of the school.
