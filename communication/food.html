SCENARIO 2

Service vs. Food Quality

🍔 You visited a new restaurant. The chicken hamburger was juicy and delicious, the atmosphere was cozy — BUT the service was too slow. You waited 40 minutes! What do you say?
FreshTasty / FlavorfulRelaxing Beautifully decoratedAnnoyingUnprofessionalDisappointed
🗣️ Speaking Scaffolding

Opener: "I recently visited this restaurant. In my opinion, the food is and the atmosphere is ."

Negative point: "However, the service is . I feel because I had to wait for 40 minutes."

Decision: "Will I return? Yes / No, because ."

💬 "I recently visited this restaurant. In my opinion, the food is juicy and delicious and the atmosphere is cozy. However, the service is too slow. I feel disappointed because I had to wait for 40 minutes. Will I return? Yes, because the food is worth it — but I hope they improve their service speed."
Part 5 · Pronunciation

Plural -s / -es Endings 🔊

Focus on the highlighted word, tap 🔊 to listen, then repeat aloud!

The rule

/s/ after voiceless sounds — types, carrots  ·  /z/ after voiced sounds — vitamins, vegetables  ·  /ɪz/ after s, z, ʃ, tʃ, dʒ — classes, pieces
